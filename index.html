Yanhong Zhao (赵艳红)

I am an artist hailing from Jingdezhen, Jiangxi Province, China, with a specialization in ceramics. I graduated with a degree in Sculpture from Jingdezhen Ceramics University in 2019. I am living in Florence, Italy. And I finished my Italian language course from Università per Stranieri di Siena recently. My art work primarily focuses on ceramics, using sculpture to explore and comment on contemporary social issues. In addition to sculpture, I am deeply passionate about photography and video art, which I often incorporate into my creations to enhance the depth and diversity of my artistic expression.

Activities
  • 2024/06: I joined the Workshop "Planting and adopting plants!", Palazzi Community Center, Florence, Italy.
  • 2024/04: I joined Biennale Artbox EXPO Venice 2.0 with my artwork "Incantesimo del Sonno", ARTBOX.Groups GmbH, Venice, Italy.
  • 2024/04: I joined the Workshop "What if you stepped inside?", Centro per l'arte contemporanea Luigi Pecci, Prato, Italy.
